Andres Iniesta Tribute

Andres Iniesta

El Ilusionista

Achievements

  • 2002: Joined the famed La Masia academy, where he was amongst the youngest players to board.
  • 2008: Winner of the Euro's which he won again in 2012, receiving the MVP trophy.
  • 2010: Winner of the World Cup where he scored the winning goal in the final and received the MOTM award.
  • 2015: Winner of the Treble for second time under Luis Enrique.
  • 2015: Received standing ovation from Santiago Bernabeu as captain.

“Technically he is perfect and he plays so naturally, almost without effort. It’s like when Roger Federer plays tennis, he barely sweats.”
Vicente Del Bosque
